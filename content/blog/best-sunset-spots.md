There's a particular quality to the light at Shoal Bay in the late afternoon. The sun drops behind Tomaree, the bay turns gold, and for about fifteen minutes the whole place looks like a postcard. Here are the spots worth pointing yourself towards.

## The Soul Bliss Deck

Let's start at home. The upper deck at Soul Bliss faces west towards Tomaree, and on clear evenings the sun drops behind the headland in a way that's genuinely hard to describe. Pour something cold, put your feet up, and watch the light change. No effort required.

## Tomaree Head Summit

For a big sky sunset, nothing beats the summit. The 360-degree views mean you can watch the sun go down over the Tomago wetlands to the west while the last pink light lingers over the Pacific to the east. Allow an hour for the walk up and come down before full dark — bring a torch just in case.

## Fly Point, Shoal Bay

The foreshore path around Fly Point gives you a low, open view across the bay. It's perfect for photography — you get the water, the headland, and the sky in one frame. A popular spot with locals and very easy to reach on foot from Soul Bliss.

## Anna Bay Beach

Drive 20 minutes west to Anna Bay and you get full ocean sunset over one of the quieter beaches on this stretch of coast. The beach faces northwest, which means the sky behind you lights up long after the sun has gone. The nearby Stockton sand dunes turn brilliant orange in the last light.

## Nelson Bay Marina

For a sunset with company, the Marina area in Nelson Bay has cafés and restaurants with water views. The d'Albora Marina boardwalk is a nice spot to walk with a drink as the boats come in.

## Tips

- Check the weather at about 4pm — a few clouds on the horizon usually means a more dramatic sunset than a clear sky
- The best light is often in the 10–15 minutes after the sun sets (the "blue hour" that follows)
- Winter sunsets (June–August) tend to be the most dramatic, with colder, cleaner air
- In summer, the sun sets further south and slightly later — check sunset times on your phone app
